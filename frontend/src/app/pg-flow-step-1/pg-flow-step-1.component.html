Non-commercial Permit

For a non-commercial activity involving a group of 75 or more people, either as participants or spectators. Non-commercial means there’s no entry/participation fee and the primary purpose of the event isn’t selling goods or services.

Noncommercial Permit

Temporary Outfitter’s Permit

For outfitters & guides, temporary use permits allow short-term, non-renewable outfitting and guiding in increments of 50 service days, up to a maximum of 200 service days in a 180-day period.
