Special Uses Permit Applications

Non-Commercial Group Use Permit

  • A free permit for activities of 75 or more people
  • No entry fees can be charged
  • Primary purpose isn't selling goods or services
  • Application Time: Appx. 20-30 minutes
  • You will need: Event details such as date, time, etc.

Temporary Outfitting and Guiding Permit

  • For outfitters and guides who charge a fee
  • Short-term, non renewable
  • Application Time: Appx. 1 hour
  • You may need: Certificate of good standing or equivelant, operating plan, liability insurance and more (see "Learn More")

The Forest Service is a multi-faceted agency that protects 154 national forests and 20 grasslands in 43 states and Puerto Rico. Our mission is to sustain the health, diversity, and productivity of the nation's forests and grasslands to meet the needs of present and future generations.
